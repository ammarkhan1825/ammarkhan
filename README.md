# 👋 Hello, I'm Ammar Khan
🎓 I am a PhD Student at Northwestern Polytechnical University (NPU), China.  
🌊 My research field is Design of Underwater Platforms.  
⚙️ My primary research focus is Hydrodynamic Design of Underwater Platforms.  
🚀 Currently, I am working on "Disc-Shaped Autonomous Underwater Vehicles (AUVs)".

## 🔬 Research Interests
- Hydrodynamic performance analysis
- CFD simulation (RANS-based modeling)
- Shape optimization of underwater vehicles
- Ground (seabed) effect modeling
- Surrogate modeling (GPR / Kriging)
- Multi-objective optimization (NSGA-II)
- Design parameter reduction techniques

## 🧠 Current Research Focus
My current research investigates:
- Hydrodynamic characteristics of disc-shaped AUVs  
- Drag and lift behavior under different operating conditions  
- Flow separation and wake structure analysis  
- Efficient surrogate-based optimization frameworks  
- Design improvement strategies for enhanced performance
